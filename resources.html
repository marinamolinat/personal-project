Cool resources to learn about cybersecurity

Cs50x cybersecurity

Cs50x cybersecurity is a course by Harvard for an introduction in cybersecurity. It's completely free with awesome lectures and a certificate. Its a way a nice way to learn more, though it's long and not for everyone. Site

Phishing.org

A simple page that talks about phishing attacks and scams, and how to avoid them. Site

Stay safe online

An organisation with the purpose of teaching others about online safety. It has plenty resources about different topics. Site

Darknet Diaries

If podcasts are more your thing, and you wish to learn while being entertained, this podcast can be a great option. Each episode, the narrator tells us about stories ranging from hacks, cybersecurity, and true crime. Its fun and educational. Site
